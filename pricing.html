Additional Information

  • All packages include 30 minutes for equipment setup and clear-up as standard.
  • On-site survey time is exclusive of setup/clear-up—your 2 hours is all field time.
  • Up to 120 miles total travel included at £0.45/mile; additional mileage charged at £0.45/mile.
  • Dual Deployment: Technician ensures on-site setup for both kits; analysis is post-survey only.
  • Dawn or unsociable hours: +£40 surcharge per event.
  • Overnight stays required for sites over 90 minutes from Newbury; additional cost applies.
  • Discounted bundles available for multi-phase or long-term projects.
  • All analysis assumes up to 2 hrs data per device per survey. Additional/complex data: £35/hr.
  • You pay for accuracy, not corner cutting. Every survey and review delivered to the highest professional standard.
  • High-resolution thermal footage (e.g., FLIR) may require large file transfers; hard drive exchange may be needed.
