The EchoSight Difference

At EchoSight, bat emergence surveys no longer mean hours of noisy footage. Most tools leave you sifting through branches and insects searching for real activity. EchoSight Tracker takes a smarter approach:

  • Comprehensive Capture, Smart Filtering: All motion is detected then visual noise—from swaying branches to raindrops—is stripped away.
  • Professional Ecologist Review: Every filtered clip is personally reviewed by a licensed bat ecologist for reliable interpretation.
  • End-to-End Expertise: Whether we record on-site or analyse your footage, the process and oversight are the same.

EchoSight Tracker: How It Works

  • Total Motion Capture: No activity missed in your survey footage.
  • Noise Reduction: Repetitive, irrelevant motion is removed so only meaningful events remain.
  • Expert Validation: Each clip is annotated and explained by a bat ecologist who understands your objectives.
  • Precise Timestamps & Highlight Reels: Receive concise reels with exact timestamps for easy reference.
  • Flight Path & Emergence Mapping: See clear overlays of emergence and re-entry points with flight paths.
  • Key Behaviour Capture: Emergence, re-entry and foraging are marked on frames ready for your report.
  • Integrated Acoustic ID (optional): When audio is provided, species-level analysis is integrated for defensible results.

What You Get

  • Clean, annotated highlight reels focused solely on bat activity.
  • Accurate timestamps and event logs for seamless reporting.
  • Mapped emergence and flight pathways on annotated stills and video.
  • Ready-to-insert visuals for regulatory and client-facing reports.
