On-Site Bat Surveys

Standard and premium packages, with or without your own equipment. Immediate on-site identification, professional field notes, and rapid reporting.

How it Works:

  1. Scheduling: You pick the date, we handle logistics. (Your kit or ours? Your call.)
  2. Arrival & Setup: EchoSight team arrives 30 minutes early—prepped, loaded, ready to roll.
  3. Survey: Phase 2 bat emergence survey (up to 2 hours on-site; extra time £35/hr).
    • Live acoustic ID as events unfold—real-time expertise, not guesswork.
    • Professional behavioural notes and sharp observations logged throughout.
  4. Summary: Immediate written summary before we leave—no waiting, no wondering.
  5. Data Handover:
    • Using your equipment? We'll hand over notes and results on the spot.
    • Using EchoSight gear? High-res files via secure OneDrive link—usually same-day.
  6. Upgrades: Opted for acoustic or video analysis? See below for your next steps.

Acoustic Analysis

Comprehensive review of full-spectrum recordings for accurate species ID. Upload your audio or pair with our surveys.

How it Works:

  1. Secure Upload: You or EchoSight upload acoustic files to our secure portal.
  2. Automated Screening: Software filters noise for clean call sequences.
  3. Expert Review: Licensed ecologist confirms species and behaviour.
  4. Species Log: Key calls logged with notes for your report.
  5. Delivery: Annotated files and concise summary via secure download.
  6. Follow-up: Optional consultation on interpretation and next steps.
