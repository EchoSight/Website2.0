Standard and premium packages, with or without your own equipment. Immediate on-site identification, professional field notes, and rapid reporting.

    • Using your equipment? We'll hand over notes and results on the spot.

Acoustic Analysis

Manual, expert review of full-spectrum recordings for confident, defensible species identification. Upload your audio or pair with our survey services.

How it Works:

  1. Secure Upload: You or EchoSight upload acoustic files to our secure portal.
  2. Manual Call Review: Every sequence is assessed in detail by an experienced ecologist—no automation, just expertise.
  3. Expert Identification: Species and behaviour are confirmed and annotated by a licensed bat specialist.
  4. Species Log: Key calls and findings are logged with notes tailored for your report.
  5. Delivery: Receive annotated files and a concise, actionable summary via secure download.
  6. Follow-up: Optional consultation to support interpretation and next steps for your project.

Video Emergence & Re-entry Analysis

Our Equipment

Our kit is carefully chosen for clarity and reliability in the field. Here's what keeps us efficient.

Thermal Detection

We use the Pixfra A625 LRF Pro. Advanced thermal imaging camera with integrated laser rangefinder. Features a highly sensitive sensor (NETD ≤ 18 mK), crisp 640x512 resolution, and smooth 50Hz framerate—enabling precise, real-time detection and distance measurement of bats even in complete darkness. Perfect for accurate roost identification during low-light and night-time surveys.

Coffee fuels our long nights and a comfy chair keeps us focused.

Acoustic Detection

The Elekon Batlogger M2 offers high-sensitivity full-spectrum recording (10–192 kHz) with selectable sampling rates up to 500 kHz, built-in GPS geotagging, environmental data logging, and live monitoring via speaker or earphones. Features include adjustable triggers, scheduled recording, voice notes, and up to 15 hours runtime for detailed, reliable acoustic surveys.

The EchoMeter Touch Pro 2 is our compact backup detector compatible with iOS/Android, providing real-time species suggestion and additional survey support should any issues arise.
