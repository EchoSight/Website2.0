The EchoSight Difference

EchoSight Tracker aims to set a new standard for bat survey video analysis, delivering precise results without the internal burden of manual review. Our advanced review pipelines and system intelligently filters out non-essential movement, ensuring that your team can focus exclusively on meaningful activity. Select the service level that matches your requirements:

  • Total Motion Capture, Intelligent Filtering: All movement is detected and processed, with only genuine wildlife activity retained for review—eliminating unnecessary distractions.
  • Client-Led or Expert-Validated Analysis: Prefer in-house assessment? Receive fully processed clips for your own team to review. For assured accuracy, opt for our expert validation—where every clip is annotated and explained by a licensed bat ecologist.
  • Accurate Timestamps & Highlight Reels: Every significant event is clearly marked, providing rapid access to the moments that matter and providing ease when looking for that accoustic recording.
  • Flight Path and Emergence Mapping: Detailed overlays pinpoint emergence, re-entry points, and flight paths—delivering clarity and defensible evidence.
  • Behavioural Insights for Reporting: Key behaviours, including emergence, re-entry, and foraging, are tagged and readily available for your reports and project records.
  • Integrated Acoustic Identification (optional): Where audio is available, species-level identification is incorporated to enhance survey robustness.
  • On-Site or Remote Expertise: Whether EchoSight conducts the survey or analyses your footage, you benefit from the same rigorous process and professional oversight.
